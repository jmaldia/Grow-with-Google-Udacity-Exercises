Buddy the Chipoo

Buddy is a dog who is half Chihuahua and half miniature Poodle.

  • Favorite drink: Water
  • Favorite snack: Turkey
  • Loves to: Go outside and play
  • Personality: Moody

Buddy loves to cuddle with his owner, however barks at every stranger that comes into his house! He is very protective of his family and loves his human brothers and sisters.
